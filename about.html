For the
Passionate few

Welcome to my website! I'm Tom, a software developer, but also I am
so much more than that. I am a poet, a lover, an entrepeneur, a
creative, and even at times a buddhist monk.

I aspire to live a life of devotion, simplicity, contribution and growth. I have two core practices in my life, meditation, and software development, and they both feed into one another. Life is about discipline and self mastery. It is not about what you can get, it is about what you can give. It is the journey before the destination, the means before the ends. The process is everything and I am in a continual state of self improvement, intellectually, emotionally, spiritually, physically I am training myself to be the best and greatest I can possibly be. My hope is that I can inspire you through my accomplishments, achievements, joy, happiness, kindness and compassion.

Professionally I am dedicated to software development as my craft, it is a sacred process, a means by which I can contribute to the world, generate a measurable impact, and access states of deep flow and concentration.

As a hobby I now also write and perform poetry/spoken word, you can purchase a book containing my work here. The money goes to Mind UK, a mental health charity, a cause close to my heart. Please feel free to donate if you believe in this cause. I have suffered through depression before and was lucky enough to discover and transform my life through meditation. You can read about this here. Now I enjoy my days writing software, and have recently been hired by a start-up called Twine.
